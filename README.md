01 | PROJECT OVERVIEW
This project, conducted as part of the Digital Literacy curriculum at VIT Bhopal, focuses on the effective and responsible use of digital tools in both academic and professional spheres.
Throughout this course, I explored the essential pillars of the modern digital landscape:
•	Building a professional online presence.
•	Mastering technical collaboration platforms.
•	Practicing high-standard email etiquette.
•	Developing a robust understanding of cyber safety.
 
02 | TASK SUMMARIES
Task 1: Digital Literacy Infographic
I designed a visual guide using Canva to distill complex digital literacy concepts into an accessible format. Key focus areas included safe internet practices and essential academic tools.
Outcome: Mastered visual storytelling and information hierarchy.
Task 2: Student Digital Portfolio
I established a professional foundation by optimizing my GitHub and LinkedIn profiles. This task emphasized the importance of a "digital footprint" in career networking.
Outcome: Created a bridge between academic learning and industry visibility.
Task 3: Coding & Collaboration Platforms
I engaged with HackerRank to solve foundational coding challenges and utilized the Google Workspace ecosystem to create data-collection tools (Google Forms) and response analysis sheets.
Outcome: Gained proficiency in automated data collection and technical problem-solving.
Task 4: Email Etiquette & Communication
Drafted formal correspondence for academic and corporate scenarios. I also developed a "Social Media Manifesto" containing critical do’s and don’ts for maintaining a clean online reputation.
Outcome: Refined the art of professional tone and concise communication.
Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness
I analyzed a case study on cyberbullying and developed a comprehensive prevention checklist. This task highlighted the psychological and legal aspects of digital safety.
Outcome: Enhanced defensive digital habits and threat recognition.
